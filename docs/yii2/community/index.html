Community

We are proud to have a friendly and helpful Yii community. We believe such a community will not only get you started faster and more easily with Yii, but also make the Yii programming a happy experience for you.

Forum

Join our friendly forum to discuss about everything, whether they are related to Yii or not.

Wiki

See how other people solve Yii-related problems. And share your Yii experiences with others.

Extensions

Yii has offered solid foundation in helping you write reusable code. Would you like to reuse your code at the maximum degree by sharing it in our extension repository?

IRC Channel

To get near-instant help or support with the Yii framework or just to have an enjoyable chat, try visiting #yii on the Freenode irc network. You will be surprised at seeing many people hanging around there.

You may also try our Web-based IRC terminal which will directly connect you to the channel.
